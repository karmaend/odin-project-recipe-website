Margherita Pizza Recipe

Description

A Homemade Margherita Pizza is the perfect blend of simplicity and flavor. Originating in Naples, Italy, this pizza pays homage to the Italian flag with its red tomatoes, white mozzarella, and green basil. With a crisp crust, fresh ingredients, and a rustic feel, this is how pizza was meant to be enjoyed.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce or crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: pinch of red pepper flakes

How to Make Margherita Pizza Step-by-Step

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  5. Top with slices of mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  8. Slice and serve hot!

What to Serve with Margherita Pizza

  • Caprese Salad
  • Roasted Garlic Mushrooms
  • Chilled Sparkling Lemonade
